Build a simple, clean, and mobile-friendly website for a Night School (Evening School).
This school is not a commercial business. It is a community and humanitarian education initiative designed to help people who work during the day and want to continue their education at night.

The design should feel simple, trustworthy, educational, and community-focused, not corporate or overly commercial.

Important Language Requirement

All website content must be written in Bangla (Bengali language).

This includes:

Navigation menu

Headings

Paragraphs

Buttons

Notices

Forms

All UI text

English should only be used for technical elements if necessary.

Project Overview

The website represents a Night School where students can study from Class 1 to Class 10 under the Dhaka Board curriculum.

Important facts about the school:

Classes are held at night.

The school is mainly for people who work during the day.

Anyone can enroll regardless of age.

The school provides very low-cost education.

Students can take exams similar to normal schools.

The goal is education access and social impact, not profit.
